# Simple Todo App

## Product Overview
A mobile app that helps individuals manage their daily tasks efficiently. It provides a straightforward way to create, track, and complete tasks without unnecessary complexity. Designed for anyone who wants a simple, focused tool for personal task management.

## Problem Statement
People often struggle to keep track of daily tasks and to-dos, leading to forgotten commitments and reduced productivity. Existing solutions are frequently over-complicated with features most users don't need. This app solves the problem by providing a minimal, distraction-free task management experience.

## Target Users
- **Individual User**: Needs a quick and easy way to capture tasks, track what's pending, and mark items as done. Uses the product to stay organised in their personal and professional life without the overhead of complex project management tools.

## Core Features

### Task Management
- Create a new task with a title and optional due date
- Mark a task as complete
- Delete a task from the list

### Task Viewing
- View all tasks in a single list view
- Filter tasks by status (complete or incomplete)
